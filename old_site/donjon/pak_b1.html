The first Donjon supplement, Donjon Pak B1: Men of Steel and Beasts of Terror! contains 41 friends and foes for your Donjon usage, including demon slayers, troglodytes, cloud dragons, kubera demons, and the dread behemoth.

This supplement is not only useful to a Donjon Master to challenge players with, but is chock-full of good examples of character types a player could use.
